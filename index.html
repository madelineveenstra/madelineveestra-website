Co-Founder of Wikifashion, the central resource for fashion

Wikifashion is a way for fashion lovers to catalogue brands, designers, fashion shows and all things fashion in a highly effective and collaborative way. Wikifashion will allow fashion lovers and designers alike to update clothing from season to season and to enable access to the most up-to-date information on fashion.

All members of the fashion community- from fashion lovers to designers are encouraged to contribute and edit content. Wikifashion is based in Brisbane, Australia and was founded by Madeline Veenstra (the fashion lover) and Coen Hyde (the techie).

About Madeline

In early 2010 I left the Treasury to further develop my own company, Wikifashion- the central resource for fashion. Since its launch in November 2009, it has received over 200,000 page views per month, 4500 contributors, over 3000 twitter followers, 550 instagram followers and hundreds of fashion bloggers support the site by displaying our blog badges.

My strategy to base Wikifashion in Brisbane was to help foster the Brisbane Fashion industry. We have hired interns from the QUT fashion school each year, as well as organizing and sponsoring the Brisbane Fashion Industry meet up monthly, which now has over 80 members.

Working on Wikifashion has allowed me to develop my skills in business development, public relations, digital and social media and creating marketing strategies.
